Executive Summary

One-page leadership readout — key findings and recommended actions

Bottom Line

There is an immediate revenue opportunity worth +2-3% in at least ten markets by raising conventional avocado prices 5%, because demand in those cities barely drops when prices rise. Separately, mid-tier cities (Buffalo-Rochester, Pittsburgh, Indianapolis) are growing sales 25-30% YoY and should receive more supply before competitors fill the gap.
